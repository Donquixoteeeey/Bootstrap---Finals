Date: December 15, 2023 (Friday)
Time: 3:00 PM Venue: SLEC

Auditorium, CPC Boulevard Campus This festive occasion is open to everyone, and admission is FREE! Bring your family, friends, and loved ones to share in this celebration of music, community, and the jubilant Christmas season! ✨🎉

A MAGICAL NIGHT AT THE DREAMLAND
What an incredible honor it was for the CPC Chorale to be part of the breathtaking Opening of Lights at the Sapian Dreamland 2023!

A heartfelt thank you to the Sapian Municipal Government headed by Hon. Mayor Jose O. Villanueva, for inviting us and giving us the chance to share our music with the amazing Sapianons. We would like also to thank Capiz Tourism and Cultural Affairs, led by Sir Al Tesoro, under the exemplary leadership of Hon. Gov. Fredenil H. Castro! Your support and commitment to promoting cultural richness and tourism in Capiz have not gone unnoticed.
